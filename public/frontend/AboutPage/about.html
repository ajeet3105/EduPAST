About EduPast

EduPast is a platform designed to help B.Tech students access previous mid-semester papers branch-wise and semester-wise.

Whether you're from CSE, Civil, Mechanical, or any other B.Tech stream, EduPast brings all your academic resources under one roof — accessible, organized, and free.
